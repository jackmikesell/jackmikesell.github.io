You walk into the pub and a majority of the residents turn to stare at you. You feel incredibly uncomfortable and are about to leave when a witch waves you over to her table.

She doesn't talk at all and only cackles. She places two potions in front of you. Of course you want nothing to do with this and try to get up but can't. You then look at the two potions. One looks like a mud pie but you get the feeling it will turn you into a frog. The other looks like water but something tells you it will swap your feet with entire mattresses

Do you drink the mud pie potion?

Or the potion that looks like water?

Drink both.
