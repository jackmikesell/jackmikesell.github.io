You swim towards the kelp and after weaving through the stalks, you find a mermaid. But she doesn't look happy to see you. She yells at you in a language you can't understand, and then throws a spear that only barely misses your side. She grabs another and starts swimming towards you. Out of the corner of your eye you see another spear only a few feet away. You think about grabbing the spear, but you're not sure if you want or feel the need to fight the mermaid.

Do you grab the spear and get ready for the incoming mermaid?

Or do you see if you can reason with her?
